## 1. The "Umbral Flux" System: Dynamic World States

The most headline-grabbing change in the 2026 update is the introduction of the **Umbral Flux**. Previously, the "Night" state was a binary toggle—either it was day, or the creeping darkness of Nightreign had taken over.

### How It Works
With the Umbral Flux, the world now operates on a sliding scale of corruption.
*   **Tier 1 Flux:** Minor enemies gain increased aggression.
*   **Tier 2 Flux:** Terrain begins to shift; bridges may crumble, and new, hidden paths reveal themselves in the geometry of the world.
*   **Tier 3 Flux (The Eclipse):** A total eclipse occurs. The sky turns violet, gravity is reduced by 20%, and "Ghost" enemies from previous playthroughs spawn invasively.

**Developer Note:** *"We wanted the Night to feel like a living, breathing entity that reacts to the player’s actions, rather than just a timer."*

## 2. Class Overhauls: The "Ascended" Skill Trees

For two years, players have maxed out their stats. The 2026 patch introduces the **Ascended Skill Tree**, a post-level 15 progression system available for all base game classes.

### Key Class Changes
*   **The Wretch (Ascended):** Gains the "Mimic’s Ire" ability. Upon taking fatal damage, the Wretch splits into two weaker versions of themselves for 15 seconds.
*   **The Sorcerer:** New "Gravity-Flame" spells have been added, combining the pull of gravity magic with the burst damage of fire.
*   **The Paladin (New Subclass):** A heavy faith-based tank class wielding the new "Greatshield of the Order." This class specializes in reflecting magical projectiles back at enemies.

These changes aim to diversify the meta, which has heavily favored dexterity and sorcery builds in recent seasons.

## 3. Combat Mechanics: Parrying and Posture

The 2026 update brings significant changes to the melee combat loop, specifically targeting the "turtle" strategy (blocking indefinitely).

### The Posture System
Inspired by *Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice* but adapted for the RPG format, enemies now possess a visible **Posture Meter**.
*   **Guard Counters** now deal massive posture damage.
*   **Jump Attacks** are tuned to break stance more reliably.
*   **New Mechanic:** If an enemy’s posture is broken while they are mid-attack, they take critical damage and are stunned for a longer duration.

### Weapon Updates
*   **Greatswords:** Recovery frames reduced by 10% on heavy attacks.
*   **Dual Wielding:** Stamina consumption reduced, but status effect buildup (Bleed, Frost) is slightly nerfed to balance the new speed.
*   **Daggers:** Now have increased critical hit multipliers (up from 1.4x to 1.6x).

*   **UI Overhaul:** The inventory menu now features a "Compare Stats" tooltip that highlights stat changes in green or red when hovering over new gear.
*   **Spectral Steed Upgrade:** Torrent now has a double-jump dash ability, allowing for faster traversal of the vertical map introduced in Nightreign.
