## Kickoff Times in India

Portugal is in the Western European Time Zone (WET), which is typically **4.5 to 5.5 hours behind India Standard Time (IST)** depending on Daylight Savings.

*   **Standard Kickoff (Lisbon Time):** 20:30 (8:30 PM)
*   **India Kickoff Time:** **01:00 AM (Next Day)**
    *   *Exception:* If the match is played on a Sunday afternoon in Portugal (approx. 15:00), the India time will be **19:30 (7:30 PM)**. Always check the fixture list for the specific time.

> **Pro Tip:** Since the match is often late at night, set a reminder on your phone or use the "Add to Calendar" feature on your streaming app so you don't miss the kickoff.

## VPN: Do You Need One?

Generally, **no**. If you are in India, the local broadcasting rights are held by Indian platforms (FanCode, Sony), meaning you do not need a VPN to bypass geo-restrictions.

However, if you are an Indian expat currently residing outside India (e.g., in the UK or USA) and want to access your Indian streaming account, you would need a reliable VPN set to an Indian server. **NordVPN** or **ExpressVPN** are recommended for this purpose.
