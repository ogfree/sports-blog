## Team Overview: Washington Wizards

The Wizards have been rebuilding with a focus on young talent and athleticism. Heading into 2026, expect them to lean on their backcourt duo for scoring and playmaking. Key areas to watch:

- **Offensive Strengths:** Transition offense and perimeter shooting. If their guards get hot from deep, they can stretch defenses thin.
- **Defensive Weaknesses:** Interior defense has been a vulnerability. Against a team with size like the Clippers, they'll need to tighten up in the paint.
- **Injury Watch:** Monitor any updates on their core players; depth could be a factor in a long season.

The Wizards' path to victory lies in pushing the pace and capitalizing on second-chance points. They've shown flashes of potential, but consistency remains the challenge.

## Team Overview: Los Angeles Clippers

The Clippers, perennial contenders in the West, boast a roster built for playoff runs. In 2026, their veteran leadership and star power will be on full display. Here's the scoop:

- **Offensive Strengths:** Isolation scoring and mid-range mastery. Their forwards can create their own shots, making them tough to guard one-on-one.
- **Defensive Edge:** Switch-heavy schemes and rebounding dominance. They excel at forcing turnovers and limiting opponent efficiency.
- **Potential Hurdles:** Age and health of key stars could impact fatigue, especially in back-to-backs.

For the Clippers, controlling the tempo and exploiting mismatches will be crucial. They're favored in this matchup for a reason, but the Wizards' youth could catch them off guard.
