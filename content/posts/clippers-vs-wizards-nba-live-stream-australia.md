## Kick-Off Times: When to Watch in Australia

Timing is everything when it comes to NBA basketball. Most games take place in the evening in the US, which translates to early morning viewing Down Under.

*   **Standard Tip-Off Time:** Games usually tip-off at 7:00 PM or 7:30 PM Pacific Time (US).
*   **Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST):** This typically converts to **12:00 PM (Noon)** or **1:00 PM** the following day for viewers on the East Coast of Australia.
*   **Perth (AWST):** Add 3 hours to AEST, meaning the game usually starts around **3:00 PM** or **4:00 PM**.

*Note: Always double-check the specific schedule on your streaming service or the NBA app, as tip-off times can vary slightly based on broadcast schedules and doubleheaders.*
