## Connectivity and Troubleshooting

If you face buffering or lag during the live stream, try these tips:

*   **Switch to a 5GHz Wi-Fi band:** If your router supports it, switch to the 5GHz frequency for faster speeds.
*   **Close Background Apps:** Ensure no other apps are downloading updates in the background.
*   **Clear Cache:** If the streaming app is lagging, clear its cache in your phone settings.
*   **Check Signal:** If watching on TV, ensure your DTH signal is strong.
