## Team Overview and Recent Form

The Flyers have been on an upward trajectory, focusing on a blend of young talent and veteran leadership. Under their current coaching staff, Philadelphia emphasizes a high-pressure forecheck and opportunistic scoring. In the 2025-26 season, they've shown resilience, particularly on home ice at the Wells Fargo Center. Key players like Travis Konecny and Sean Couturier have been offensive catalysts, while goalie Carter Hart has anchored the net with a solid save percentage hovering around .915.

### Buffalo Sabres: Youthful Energy and Offensive Firepower
