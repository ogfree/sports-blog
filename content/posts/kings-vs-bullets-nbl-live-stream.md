# Kings vs Bullets: NBL Live Stream Guide for Australian Fans 2026

**Updated for the 2026 NBL Season**

## How to Watch Kings vs Bullets Live in Australia

In Australia, the National Basketball League (NBL) has a dedicated broadcast and streaming partnership. To watch every Kings vs Bullets matchup—including regular season games and potential finals—you need access to the official rights holders.

### 1. Main Broadcast Partner: ESPN Australia
ESPN holds the rights to broadcast live NBL games in Australia.
*   **Where to watch:** You can stream ESPN via **Kayo Sports** or **Foxtel**.
*   **Best for:** High-quality streams, expert analysis, and multi-screen viewing.
*   **Device Compatibility:** Smart TVs, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones.

### 2. Free-to-Air: 10 Peach (Network 10)
Network 10 typically broadcasts one marquee game per round live on **10 Peach** and streaming on **10 Play**.
*   **Where to watch:** 10 Play (Website or App).
*   **Best for:** Casual fans looking for free access to select high-profile matchups.
*   **Note:** Not *every* Kings vs Bullets game will be on 10 Peach; check the weekly schedule.

### 3. NBL Official Streaming: NBL TV
While NBL TV (via the NBL app) is the official home of the league, geo-restrictions apply for Australian viewers due to broadcast rights.
*   **For Australians:** Use NBL TV only if you are traveling outside of Australia. If you are in Australia, use Kayo, Foxtel, or 10 Play.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Is the NBL free to watch in Australia?**
No, not entirely. While one game per round is usually free-to-air on **10 Peach**, most games (including every Kings vs Bullets match) require a subscription to **Kayo Sports** or **Foxtel**.

**Can I stream Kings vs Bullets for free?**
Yes, if the specific game is broadcast on **10 Play**. If you are a new user, you can also use the **7-day free trial** offered by Kayo Sports to watch the game for free.

**What time do NBL games start?**
NBL games are typically played in the evening. Tip-off times vary but usually fall between 5:30 PM and 8:30 PM AEST/AEDT, depending on the day of the week and location.

**Can I watch on multiple devices?**
Yes. **Kayo Sports** allows 3 simultaneous streams per account. **Foxtel** allows 2 streams via the Foxtel Go app.
